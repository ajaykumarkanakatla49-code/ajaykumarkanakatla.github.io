About Me

I'm a final-year engineering student who completed an industrial placement as a Project Coordinator on a real railway infrastructure scheme. I led a team of six engineers to design a subway tunnel and station platforms — working under the pressure of a live, operational train station.

My role spanned the full project lifecycle: from setting up the programme and drafting the Basis of Design, through coordinating three distinct design concepts, to integrating risk assessments, sustainability evaluations, and CDM compliance into every stage of delivery.

I'm looking to build a career in project management within the infrastructure and civil engineering sector — where technical understanding and people coordination combine to deliver real-world impact.

Design of a Subway & Platforms at a Train Station

A subway constructed to facilitate pedestrian access between either side of the station and its platforms. The station remained live throughout — all structural work was scoped and sequenced to be delivered within a single 96-hour track possession window.
